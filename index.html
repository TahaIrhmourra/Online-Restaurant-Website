about us

2017

We started this project with a simple yet ambitious dream to share our cooking passion with the world.

2019

Was a year of growth and innovation. We introduced our multiple new dishes, We also expanded our menu to cater to diverse tastes, offering a range of vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options.

2020

Was presented unexpected challenges, but we persevered. We adapted to the changing landscape by introducing contactless delivery and curbside pickup, ensuring the safety and convenience of our customers.

2023

Marked our 6th anniversary, and we celebrated with a special event that showcased our culinary journey. We also Reached our 100K customer.

2024

We're excited to continue our journey in making the most delicious dishes, We're also embracing sustainable practices to reduce our environmental impact, ensuring a greener future for generations to come.
